How to join NIIT

NIIT technology usually conducts 3 rounds to filter out the Candidates for the profile of software engineer. There was on the spot registration process for the drive on the basis of first come first serves. They entertained around 600 people and afterward stopped further registrations. Post registration, we gathered for small pre placement talk in Sviet auditorium where NIIT officials explained the job profile, role and salary bteakup etc.First round was offline MCQ based paper having aptitude, reasoning, English and few basic technical question which were easy to attempt. One thing I would like to jot down that cut off was much higher than I expected, they were selecting only few candidates an All the qualified candidates got message for the second round (technical interview ) which was scheduled next day. Out of 600 candidates only 50 people were called for technical round and last round was HR round which didn't happen.Finally they selected 16 candidates and one group picture was taken of all the selected candidates with college director and company Officials.
